Share collection — GO-LIVE v1

Internal + verified external. Public/open is roadmap (SCR-129).

1 · Distribution, not review

Dropped Commenter / Approver roles and annotate/comment toggles from the previewer share. Collection capabilities are View / View & download only — you browse and pull a 100-asset set, you don't mark it up.

2 · Two scopes at go-live → list glyph

Internal (account access) reads as the people glyph; External — verified guests reads as the globe glyph (it's left the org). The share action sets the collection's reach state. Public / open is roadmap — it needs the rights-cleared gallery + CDN-mint (SCR-129), so it's not a scope option yet.

3 · Rights enforced per-asset at delivery

No collection-level rights gate. The delivery/mint endpoint checks each asset by role (§8 row 83 "Based on rights") — restricted assets stay ADM/DM/APR only, expired/embargoed are blocked — and that cascades identically into in-app views, collections, and external shares. One enforcement point, not three.

4 · Security on the link, never the CDN

Restricted: email-code (OTP) default / password optional, expiry required (7d), watermark on, notify-on-open, one-click revoke. OTP earns its place — it gives per-recipient identity, which is what powers the dynamic watermark and named activity log. The CDN only delivers signed, short-TTL bytes behind the gate.

⚑ Go-live scope Ships now: invite (internal + verified external) and the verified-external link, with the security stack above. Public / open link is deferred to the rights-cleared gallery + CDN-mint architecture (SCR-129) and lives in the roadmap mock. §7 row 73 ("Create public share links") should be reworded to "external share links (verified)" to match what go-live actually ships.
